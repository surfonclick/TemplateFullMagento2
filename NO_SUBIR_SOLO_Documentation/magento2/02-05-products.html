1. Related/Upsell/Crossell Products

Back to top

To add related products for a product, navigate to Products > Catalog > select product to edit, click on ADVANCED SETTINGS Related Products tab

You can do similar as below steps for Cross-sells and Up-sell products

Filter to show visible products. Check related products and click on 'Save' or 'Save and Continue Edit' button

2. Display Product Options

Back to top

You can change position of options in product page layout (for products which have options). These options can be displayed below the product information (name, short description, ...) or below the product image. To configure this, go to Product > Catalog, select product to edit, in ADVANCED SETTINGS Design tab, select value for 'Display Product Options in'.
